About Me

I’m an undergraduate researcher at the University of Virginia working at the intersection of robot learning, and AI. My experience spans teleoperation of the Franka Emika Panda arm, autonomous navigation and excavation for the NASA Lunabotics Challenge, reinforcement learning for physically grounded human-AI collaboratio, and LLM guided RL in autonomous highway driving.

I am passionate about building intelligent systems that collaborate with humans, especially in challenging real-world environments such as smart homes, decentralized robotics, and planetary exploration.

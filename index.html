About Me

I’m an undergraduate researcher at the University of Virginia working at the intersection of robotics, autonomy, and human-centered AI. My experience spans teleoperation of the Franka Emika Panda arm, autonomous navigation and excavation for the NASA Lunabotics Challenge, and reinforcement learning for physically grounded human-AI collaboration in Professor Yen-Ling Kuo’s lab.

I am passionate about building intelligent systems that collaborate with humans, especially in challenging real-world environments such as smart homes, decentralized robotics, and planetary exploration.

What I’m Working On

  • Teleoperation and shared autonomy for the Franka robotic arm
  • Human-AI collaboration models for long-horizon tasks
  • Multi-robot navigation and real-time decision making
  • UVA MARS NASA Lunabotics robotics system development
